# Xiangqi Opening Book

Play these lines when they match the position.

## As Red (first mover)

| Opening | Moves | Idea |
|---------|-------|------|
| 中炮 Central Cannon | h2e2 | Cannon to center, most popular and aggressive |
| 飞相局 Elephant Opening | c0e2 | Solid defense-first, flexible development |
| 仙人指路 Pawn Opening | g3g4 or c3c4 | Probe opponent's response, flexible |
| 起马局 Knight Opening | h0g2 | Develop knight first, positional |
| 过宫炮 Palace Cannon | h2f2 | Cannon inside palace, unusual but sound |
| 士角炮 Advisor-Corner Cannon | h2d2 | Cannon to advisor corner, tricky |

## As Black (responding)

| Against | Response | Moves | Idea |
|---------|----------|-------|------|
| Central Cannon | 屏风马 Screen Knights | b9c7, h9g7 | Both knights developed, solid defense |
| Central Cannon | 顺手炮 Same-Direction Cannon | h7e7 | Counter-cannon, aggressive |
| Central Cannon | 列手炮 Opposite Cannon | b7e7 | Mirror cannon, tactical |
| Central Cannon | 反宫马 Reversed Knight | h9g7, b9a7 | Asymmetric knights, modern |
| Elephant Opening | 中炮 Counter Central Cannon | b7e7 | Take initiative as Black |

## Development Principles

- 中炮 + 馬 is the most common setup (first 3 moves)
- Develop both knights before moving pawns
- Don't move 象/仕 without reason — they guard the King
- 車 should find open files early
- 炮 is strongest in the opening (many pieces to jump over)
- 兵 crossing the river becomes a real threat — push when safe

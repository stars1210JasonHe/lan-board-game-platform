# Chess Opening Book

Play these lines when they match the position. Prefer openings you're already in.

## As White

| Opening | Moves | Idea |
|---------|-------|------|
| Italian Game | 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4 | Target f7, fast development |
| Ruy Lopez | 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5 | Pressure e5 pawn, solid |
| Scotch | 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.d4 | Open center early |
| Queen's Gambit | 1.d4 d5 2.c4 | Fight for center, offer pawn |
| London System | 1.d4 Nf6 2.Nf3 e6 3.Bf4 | Solid, easy to play |
| English | 1.c4 | Flexible, transpose later |

## General Rules

- Follow known theory for 3-5 moves, then play on principles
- If unsure, develop a piece toward the center
- If opponent deviates from book, switch to principled play
